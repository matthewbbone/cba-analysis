Section 7.    Payroll Deductions and Dues

It is agreed that ALADS' dues and such other deductions as may be properly requested and lawfully permitted shall be deducted monthly by Management from the salary of each employee covered hereby who files with the County a written authorization within the provisions of applicable State law. Remittance of the aggregate amount of all dues and other proper deductions made from the salaries of employees covered hereunder shall be made to ALADS by Management within thirty (30) working days after the conclusion of the month in which said dues and deductions were deducted.

ALADS agrees to indemnify and hold the County of Los Angeles harmless from any liabilities of any nature which may arise as a result of the application of the provisions of this Article.

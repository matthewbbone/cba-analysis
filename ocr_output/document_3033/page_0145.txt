DEPUTY SHERIFF - BONUS I POSITIONS

Definition

Specified jobs within the Deputy Sheriff classification shall be designated as Bonus Level I positions and a salary Bonus of 5 \( \frac{1}{2} \% \) above the salary step of the employee appointed shall be awarded during his or her assignment to these positions. (Refer to the Bonus Selection Standards Manual for complete lists of all designated Bonus Level positions.)

Current Deputy Sheriff IV's shall not receive this Bonus in addition to their Deputy IV pay. Should they reduce to Deputy Sheriff Generalist, they would become eligible to receive the Bonus pay for this level.

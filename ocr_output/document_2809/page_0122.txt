Restoration of Positions and Hours

8. No work regularly performed within the Unit shall be performed outside the unit except by CSW's or substitutes who fall within the definitions listed in this agreement. The District must maintain and permit CSEA to inspect copies of registration forms and transcripts to prove the number of course units enrolled in and for which credit was received by each CSW each fall and spring semester or equivalent.

9. All hiring and changes of hours of paraeducators, CSW's or substitutes and all layoffs, bumps and transfers of paraeducators shall be controlled by the District's central office, subject to negotiations with CSEA, where applicable, and approval by the Board of Education. No such personnel change shall be approved without identifying the classification and position.

10. Upon request, the District shall furnish CSEA with listings of all paraeducators and CSW's employed by the District showing each worker's classification and position equivalent, if any, and work location. Position numbers will also be furnished when they become available.

11. If a Unit position is reduced or eliminated and the work must continue to be performed, or the work is reinstated, it shall be assigned only to workers in the Unit. In no event shall this work be performed by volunteers, so-called hourly workers, CSW's or workers in other units, nor shall any paraeducator be offered hourly employment in order to avoid being laid off, reduced in hours or displaced. Laid off paraeducators will not be replaced with CSW's, and existing Unit positions that become vacant will not be filled with CSW's.

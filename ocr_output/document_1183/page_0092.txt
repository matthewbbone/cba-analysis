ARTICLE 9 - DISCHARGE AND DISCIPLINARY ACTION:

The Company agreed that a Union Steward would be involved by Management for any meetings with employees concerning job performance problems, or in investigatory interviews which could potentially lead to discipline.

ARTICLE 10 - WORKWEEK AND HOURS:
Section I: 4-10 Work Week
Normal Workweek will include four (4) ten (10) hour days at standard workweek.
Monday - Thursday

(Daily Report-in pay. The report-in will be eight (8) hours on days constituting normal work days and four (4) hours for work on the 5th, 6th, and 7th days of the workweek.

Premium Pay: All hours worked in excess of ten (10) in a normal workweek shall be compensated at one and one-half times his straight hourly rate. All hours worked in excess of ten (10) hours on any day during the week shall be compensated at two (2) times his straight hourly rate.

Providing the employee has worked all hours available to him, work performed on the 5th (1st scheduled day off), and 6th (2nd scheduled day off) will be at the 1/2 times normal straight hourly rate.
All hours performed on the 7th day (3rd scheduled day off) will be paid twice (2) times the normal straight hourly rate.

Holiday Pay: Payment will be made on a normal ten (10) hour workweek basis if holiday should fall within their scheduled workweek. If holiday should fall on other than normal workweek, payment will be made on eight (8) hour basis; Schedule permitting; holiday that occur on a Friday or Saturday will be celebrated on the preceding Thursday.

General Pay: Payment will be made on a normal ten (10) hour work week.

Paychecks Delivered on Thursday.

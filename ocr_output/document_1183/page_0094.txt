ARTICLE 14: HEALTH & SAFETY

Section 2: First Aid:

The Company states that in the event of injury or sickness, they will be utilized to fill-in for Industrial Health Supervisor.

Section 3: Wearing Apparel:

In recognition of the need for certain insulated bibs in Boiler/Refrigeration to have appropriate protection while working in the freezer area, the Company indicated a willingness to provide insulated coveralls and/or insulated bib and coat if it's chosen by the affected employees. The current stock of insulated bibs will be used until they are replaced. If an employee chooses not to wear their own insulated apparel, the Company will provide them with insulated apparel at no cost. Insulated bib and coat are returned at the same time or upon termination of employment. If not returned, the value of the apparel will be deducted from the employee last pay check.

The Company agreed to purchase freezer suits for employees required to wear such items at 100% every 2 years from date of issue provided that used apparel is retained at the same time or upon termination of employment if not secured, the value of the apparel will be deducted from the employee last pay check.

The Company agrees to reimburse up to a maximum of $100.00 for purchase of OSHA approved prescription glasses.

Safety, Frames and Lenses once a year for basic maintenance; employees required to wear prescription glasses.

Section 5: Safety Committee:

Safety Committee Meetings will be held once a month. A notice of the meeting date will be sent by the Industrial Health Supervisor. One (1) steward per Union on each shift will be invited to Safety Committee Meetings and such stewards will serve on such committees for up to six (6) months with rotation of stewards to follow. Minutes of the meetings will be sent to the Union Business Agent; a status update of safety items will be included in the Minutes. Copies of the Safety Minutes will be made available through the Health Services Department. Response to reported safety-related items in the Plant will be done as quickly as possible.

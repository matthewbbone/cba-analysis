Section 6.- Temporary Vacancy: In case of any temporary vacancy, resulting from absence, leave of absence, vacation or an emergency such vacancy will be filled as follows:

1. All permanent employees will be given the opportunity to sign an interest sheet to be placed on two upgrade jobs within their own work area and shift on a seniority basis. Upgrade jobs are defined as those job above entry level rate of pay. To be eligible to sign an interest sheet, the job must be at the same or higher rate of pay than the employee's current position.

2. When such vacancy occurs in the "apprentice" classified positions (i.e., Dummy Mixer, day shift), the seniority qualified person in the day shift will fill-in until he is replaced.

3) The resulting vacancy, if scheduling warrants it, replacement will be filled by:

A. the least senior available entry-level in that work area; or

B. the least senior available entry-level in another work area.

This provision will provide that displaced employees an opportunity to work in higher "old" job classification on a temporary vacancy basis.

4) In case such vacancy exceeds fourteen (14) working days, the vacancy shall be filled in accordance with the provisions of Section 5 of this Article; provided, however, that a vacancy occasioned by sickness, vacation or leave of absence shall not be subject to bid.

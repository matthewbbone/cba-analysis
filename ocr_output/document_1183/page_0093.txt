Section 2. Overtime:

No change in how daily overtime is administered. Weekend overtime will be offered to the employee within the classification of the Department (Production) Department will be subject by classification when work needs a high senior employee will be classified first. If there are enough employees available from their respective order for the entry level work (for Production, employee this is within the work area). Prior to drifting of Entry Level employee displaced classified employees from the Department will be selected in seniority order for the Entry Level work (for Production, employee this is again within the work area).

The Company and Union agreed that the Warehouse Overtime Agreement effective as of 1/26/96 has worked to an efficient manner and it will be incorporated into the Manual Supplement on a permanent basis.

The Company and Union have agreed to administer end of shift work duties in the Warehouse department in accordance with the following understanding:

If, on the 1st Shift, the Company is aware by 2:00 p.m. of a needed Overtime work situation expected to be more than thirty (30) minutes in duration; the proper employee(s) will be notified by seniority order, to accommodate the Overtime work situation.

(If less than thirty (30) minutes of work is needed at the end of a shift to finish out a task or assignment; the employee performing such work during the shift will be permitted to finish out such a task or assignment).

The above understanding will also apply to a second (2nd) or third (3rd) Shift situation, being a time which corresponds to the same cut off point at 2:00 p.m. by that (1st) shift.

Section 4. Daily Report-in:

Each employee who reports for work in accordance with this schedule shall be entitled to the following minimum number of hours of work or pay in lieu thereof, at his straight time hourly rate:

a. Normal Work Day - seven (7) hour

b. Sixth (6th) and seventh (7th) day of their respective workweeks; four (4) hours.

Section 10. Starting Times:

For the Sanitation Area and the Dock Area Management indicated that the most senior employee within the area may select a more preferable starting time within the shift should a vacancy occur, prior to bringing in new employee into the Work Area.

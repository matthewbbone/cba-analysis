Section 5 - Safety Committee

A joint Labor-Management Safety Committee will be established at each plant. The Committee shall meet once a month. The Union, and employees covered by this Agreement agree to cooperate fully with management in the administration of the Company safety policies. The Company shall pay for safety equipment required by the Company, federal, and/or state regulations. Safety shoes will be furnished at cost to employees.

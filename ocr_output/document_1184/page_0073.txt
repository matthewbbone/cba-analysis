ARTICLE 15
Bonding-Shop Card-Subcontracting

Section 1 On or after July 1, 2004 all employers signatory to this agreement shall provide and be covered by a surety bond for no less than thirty thousand dollars ($30,000) for the purpose of protecting the Trust Funds as provided for in this agreement. The Joint Committee will review all applications and issue Shop Cards. The Committee shall have the authority, based upon past work and financial responsibilities, to require higher bond limits.

Section 2 Further, should an employer fail to comply with the monthly remittance reporting provision of this agreement, then such employer may be required to submit the fringe benefit report to the Local Union on a weekly basis, including his check to cover such report. This weekly report shall be due no later than the following Wednesday after the regular Friday payday.

Section 3 The employer agrees that should work, as herein defined be contracted or assigned to a subcontractor, provisions shall be made to require compliance by the subcontractor with all the terms of this agreement.

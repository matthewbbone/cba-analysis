SECTION VIII - ZONE PAY

800. ZONE 1 - The Free Zone around Las Vegas shall be within forty (40) road miles from the intersection of Charleston Boulevard and Maryland Parkway.

801. ZONE 2 - Work performed outside of the Las Vegas Area Free Zone between forty (40) to sixty (60) road miles from Maryland Parkway and Charleston shall be compensated at rate set forth in Section V of this Agreement.

802. ZONE 3 - Work performed outside of the Las Vegas Area Free Zone of over sixty (60) road miles shall be compensated at the rate set forth in Section V.

803. When the Contractor furnishes transportation to workmen to and from the jobsite on the Contractor's time, no travel and subsistence or zone pay shall be paid.

804. Workmen performing work outside Zone 1 shall receive the appropriate rate for not less than eight (8) hours per day.

805. No premium shall be paid regarding travel, subsistence or zone pay if a workman has been a bona fide resident for a period of six (6) months prior to employment and is employed in one of the areas described below:

PAHRUMP        MESQUITE
CALIENTE       ALAMO
PIOCHE         BEATTY
OVERTON        INDIAN SPRINGS
LOGANDALE      LATHROP WELLS
LAUGHLIN/BULLHEAD CITY TONOPAH

Deferred compensation is an agreement between the employer and the Vancouver School District to postpone (i.e., defer) part of the employeeâ€™s income until the employee separates from service. Employees are eligible to participate in the Deferred Compensation Program providing they are regularly scheduled to work twenty (20) hours or more per week.

183. Effective July 1, 2004, the qualifying years of service under this provision shall be lowered to twenty-three (23) years or more of service as a sworn member of the Department or Airport Bureau.

F. Experienced Officer Incentive Pay

184. To ensure each district station is adequately staffed with senior officers at night, effective July 1, 2004, the most senior officer at each district station and on each watch with twenty-three (23) or more years of service shall receive a premium in the amount equal to 2% of base pay as additional incentive to work night duty assignments, subject to the following conditions and limitations:

185. 1. Night duty assignments are defined as 2100-0700 hours (9:00pm-7:00am);

186. 2. The premium shall be limited to night duty field assignments in FOB District Stations. (Station duty and station keeper assignments shall not be eligible for Experienced Officer Incentive Pay);

187. 3. Only the ranks of police officer (Q2-Q4) and sergeant (Q50-Q52) shall be eligible to receive Experienced Officer Incentive Pay;

188. 4. If the senior officer on a watch is off-duty, then the next senior officer with twenty-three years or more of service shall be eligible;

189. 5. Employees that have been issued a suspension (whether the suspension was served or held in abeyance) in the three years immediately preceding shall not be eligible;

190. 6. Experienced Officer assignments shall be for a minimum of twelve (12) months;

191. 7. Employees shall only receive Experienced Officer Incentive Pay for actual hours worked.

192. In accordance with the provisions of Charter Section A8.597-1, this premium shall be included for purposes of retirement benefit calculations and contributions. This amount is not considered “salary attached to the rank” as defined by Charter Sections A8.595-1, A8.559-1, A8.558 and A8.544.

G. On-Call Premium

193. Employees, who as part of the duties of their positions are required by the Chief of the Department or designee to be on-call when normally off duty and to be instantly available to return to work to perform their duties, shall be paid a premium at the rate equivalent to three (3) hours of their regular base rate of pay for each assignment of on-call duty.

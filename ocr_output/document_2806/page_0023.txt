Section 16.  Courtesy Parking System for Court Attendance.

130. The Department agrees to maintain the current courtesy parking system for employees while attending court as a result of a subpoena on behalf of or in defense of the City or the Department when attendance is in the Hall of Justice.

Section 17.  District Station Parking.

131. The City will make a reasonable effort to provide adequate parking to employees at the district stations.

Section 18.  Code Book.

132. The Department shall make available to all units at least two (2) complete sets of Code Books and Department Orders which shall include, but not be limited to, the following: Penal Code, Police Code, Vehicle Code, Park Code, Health Code, Fire Code, Training Bulletins, Information Bulletins, Special Orders, General Orders. Of the two (2) complete sets, one (1) set shall be kept in the station equipment room which is accessible to the station keeper or his/her designee and made available to all employees.

133. The Department will amend DGO 3.08 (Court Appearances by Members) to allow officers working a midnight shift (a ten hour shift beginning on or after 2100), to adjust their shift hours to accommodate a court appearance on the succeeding day, provided minimum staffing levels are met (i.e., an officer may adjust his or her shift from 2200-0800 to 2300-0900 or 0001-1000).

Section 19.  Employee Training Reimbursement Program.

134. The City will contribute five thousand dollars ($5,000) annually to the Employee Tuition Reimbursement Program for the exclusive use of employees covered under this MOU.

135. Subject to available monies, an employee may submit a request for tuition reimbursement up to five-hundred dollars ($500) during each fiscal year.

Section 20.  Medal of Valor Awards Ceremony.

136. The City acknowledges the authority of the Police Commission to honor bargaining unit members with formal awards for outstanding service, above and beyond the call of duty. Such awards shall include, but are not limited to, Medals of Valor, Meritorious Conduct and Police Commission Commendations. Such awards shall be presented at an awards ceremony. The cost of each ceremony shall not exceed $3,000 and the cost per Fiscal Year shall not exceed $6,000.

Section 10. Trading Privileges.

85. An employee may trade his or her tour of duty with another employee of the same rank within his/her unit with the approval of his/her Commanding Officer, provided said trade results in no net increase in cost to the City and further provided that employees shall not exceed one trade for every two pay periods. Such trades shall be paid back within 90 days.

Section 11. Watch Sign-Up.

86. A. Employees assigned to a station or unit shall be assigned to watches according to a semi annual seniority sign-up.

B. Rules of the Sign-Ups.

87. Each unit/station will conduct two (2) seniority sign-ups per year as follows:

88. a. Commanding Officers will determine the size of each watch in advance of the sign-up.

89. b. Employees will sign up for their choice of watch in order of seniority.

90. c. The results of the Sign-Up will take effect on the first day of the first pay period in the months of March and September of each year of this MOU.

91. d. The Sign-Up period will commence thirty (30) calendar days prior to the first day of the first pay period in the months of March and September of each year of this MOU.

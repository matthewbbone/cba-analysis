4. Single Irregular Work Shift: A single irregular work shift can start any time from 5:00 pm to 1:00 am. All employees who work a single irregular work shift on governmental mandated night work shall be paid an additional $1.75 per hour. Section 5 will be effective for work bid on or after September 1, 2000. It is understood and agreed that if the single irregular work shift language is not included in the NYS Department of Labor prevailing wage rate schedules, the premium is waived.

6. Military Encampment Allowance

An Employee who is required to attend an encampment of the Reserve of the Armed Forces or the National Guard shall be paid, for a period not to exceed two (2) weeks in any one (1) calendar year, the difference between the amount paid by the government (not including travel, subsistence and quarters allowance) and his/her applicable Salary Rate of Pay plus any applicable salary differential (Red Circle) for the number of days s/he would have been scheduled to work during such encampment.

d. The bonus payment rate for a calculation period shall be the result determined by dividing the sum of all the bonus credits of all eligible Employees for the calculation period into the Service Bonus Pool for such calculation period.

e. The Service Bonus will be paid with the pay for the first pay period closed in November following the applicable calculation period.

f. No Service Bonus will be payable to any Employee for the calculation period in which the Employee quits or is discharged.

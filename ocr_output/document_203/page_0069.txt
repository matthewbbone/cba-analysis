ARTICLE TEN – PAID TIME OFF AND LEAVES OF ABSENCE

Section A. Holidays

1. An Employee shall be paid two and one-half (2½) times his/her hourly equivalent Salary Rate of Pay for all hours worked on any of the holidays specified below.
    January 1
    Martin Luther King, Jr.’s Birthday
    Good Friday
    Memorial Day
    July 4
    Labor Day
    Thanksgiving Day
    Day after Thanksgiving Day
    Day Preceding Christmas Day
    Christmas Day

2. In the event a holiday falls on Sunday, it shall be observed on Monday. A holiday is the twenty-four (24) hour period beginning at the shift-changing hour nearest to 12:01 a.m. on the day so observed.

3. Pay for a Recognized Holiday Not Worked
    a. An eligible Employee who does not work on a holiday shall be paid eight (8) times his/her hourly equivalent Salary Rate of Pay.

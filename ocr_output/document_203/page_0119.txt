1. The purpose of this Service Bonus Plan (SBP) is to provide a bonus for hours worked by eligible Employees, with the amount of bonus per hour increasing in relation to the Employee's length of continuous service.

2. Definitions
When used in this SBP or in any agreement relating thereto, the following terms are intended to have the meaning set forth below:

a. "Employees covered by this SBP" are Employees covered by the Basic Labor Agreement.

b. "Eligible Employees" are Employees covered by this SBP who have 5 or more years of continuous service as of the calculation date and who have some hours worked in the calculation period.

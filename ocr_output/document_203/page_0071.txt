b. An Employee who completes six months' service and one year's service in the same calendar year shall be entitled to receive only a total of two weeks vacation during the calendar year.

c. A week of vacation shall consist of seven (7) consecutive days.

3. Scheduling

a. On or promptly after October 1 of each year, each Employee entitled or expected to become entitled to vacation in the following year shall receive a Company form asking him/her to specify in writing the desired vacation period or periods. The Employee shall return the form to the Company within thirty (30) days.

RETIREMENT PLAN FOR HOURLY RATED EMPLOYEES
PART 03
NORTHWEST PULP AND PAPERBOARD OPERATIONS

SCHEDULE A

The operating units of the Company referred to in Section I of the Plan, each of which is defined in the Plan as a qualified unit, are the following:

1. The Cosmopolis, Longview, and Springfield branches of the Northwest Pulp and Paperboard Operations of the Company, represented by the Association of Western Pulp and Paperworkers and its affiliated Locals No. 10, 20, 211, 580, 633, and 677.

2. Such other or additional operating units of the Company as the Company may determine from time to time.

ARTICLE XVII. MISCELLANEOUS

17.1 No interest under this Plan or the Master Trust Agreement shall be assignable in anticipation of payment, either by voluntary act or by operation of law, or be liable in any way for the debts or defaults of any beneficiary, except that at the written request of a beneficiary, and subject to the Retirement Committee's approval, appropriate deductions may be made from monthly benefit payments and applied for the beneficiary's account to the payment of part of any Company group medical or hospital insurance then being carried by the beneficiary, provided that the beneficiary may at any time revoke such authorization and that the Company shall not have an enforceable interest in such assignment.

17.2 Neither the establishment, continuation, or termination of the Plan, nor anything contained herein, shall give, or be construed as giving, any Participant a right to be retained in the service of the Company as an employee.

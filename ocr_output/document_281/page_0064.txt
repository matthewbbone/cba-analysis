98.4 Call in for a single day vacation will only be permitted in the case of a plant closing.
98.5 Pay in lieu of vacation time off cannot be taken as a single day vacation.
98.6 Single day vacation cannot be granted for Saturday or Sunday (except for continuous shift departments).
98.7 If an employee takes a single day of vacation, the employee must take the remaining four days, when mutually agreeable, as single days or forfeit the time off.
98.8 Employees will not be forced to take single days of vacation for down days or holidays, but employees may collect vacation pay for single days of vacation on down days if requested in a timely manner.
98.9 A maximum of 5 single days of vacation may be used in any vacation year.

ARTICLE XIX
GROUP LIFE INSURANCE

Every employee having seniority with the Company will receive a basic life insurance policy under the Whirlpool Employee Benefits Plan. Such plan provides life insurance benefits for employees having seniority and the employee's spouse and dependents with the life insurance amounts in accordance with the employee's election in accordance with the Plan. Dependent upon the employee's election, the coverage provided may be on a contributory cost basis.
